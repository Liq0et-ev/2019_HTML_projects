RED FOX

Fast Facts: Red fox

Scientific name: Vulpes vulpes

Average weight: 3.6 to 6.8 kg

Average wingspan: 90 to 112 cm. The fox’s tail makes up one-third of its total length.

Average lifespan: 3 to 6 years

Did you know?

Red foxes are not all red. Other common red fox colours include brown, black and silver. Even foxes that come from the same litter can be different colours!
